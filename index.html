Data Deletion Policy

At Candidate.live, we respect your right to control your data. This policy explains how you can request the deletion of your personal information and account data.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and associated data by:

  • Sending an email to connect@candidate.live
  • Using the account deletion option in your dashboard settings
  • Submitting a request through our contact form

What We Delete

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your account information and profile data
  • All connections to Facebook and Instagram accounts
  • Content you've created or uploaded to our platform
  • Your preferences, settings, and activity history
  • Any stored authentication tokens and permissions

Our Deletion Process

  1. Upon receiving your deletion request, we will send a confirmation email within 24 hours.
  2. We will verify your identity to ensure the security of the deletion process.
  3. Once verified, we will begin the deletion process immediately.
  4. Complete deletion will be performed within 7 working days.
  5. You will receive a confirmation email when the deletion is complete.

Data Retention and Limitations

  • Some information may be retained in our secure backups for up to 30 days before being permanently deleted
  • Information that you have shared with other users or that others have copied may remain visible
  • Data required for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes may be retained as required by law

Additional Information

  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed
  • If you have connected social media accounts, our access to these accounts will be revoked
  • You may need to separately delete your data from integrated third-party services

Contact Information

For questions about data deletion or to submit a deletion request:

Email: connect@candidate.live
Response Time: Within 24 hours
Processing Time: Up to 7 working days
